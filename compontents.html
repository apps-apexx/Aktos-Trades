Introduction

Introducing, Ridge, the newest webflow clonable from Oakland Agency. We hope you find this useful for building out your own websites!These elements provide a base for you to edit / style towards your own projects, and also a great learning platform for new users to break down components, and understand how they are built. Please consider joining our mailing list for updates about this product and any future projects!

Be sure to install Googles Type Roboto into your webflow project, along with your favorite header type. I always recommend installing font awesome type kit so you can use symbols in your projects! Illustrations from Undraw with links provided!
